Rates are taken from DPDC's published retail tariff schedule (dpdc.gov.bd tariff page), effective . This is an estimate: the tariff sheet lists only the energy rate and demand charge, so meter rent, service charge, rebates, late-payment surcharge and any adjustment charges are not included. VAT is applied at the standard 5%. For residential (LT-A), consumers using 50 units or less are billed at the lifeline rate for all units; above 50 units, the progressive slab rates apply. Verify against your actual bill before relying on this figure.
